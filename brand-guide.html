[ 05 ] TONE_OF_VOICE

Writing Guidelines

Arb Capital communicates with institutional precision. We are technically confident without being arrogant. We let numbers and architecture speak. No hype, no superlatives, no marketing fluff.

INSTITUTIONAL_PRECISION

Write like a prime brokerage, not a startup. Factual, measured, confident. Every claim should be verifiable. Avoid exclamation marks, emojis, and casual language.

CRYPTO_NATIVE

Use protocol-native terminology: vaults, yield, alpha, TVL, APY, ERC-4626. Don't dumb it down — our audience understands DeFi mechanics. Avoid explaining basics.

CONCISE_+_DECLARATIVE

Short sentences. Active voice. Lead with the conclusion, follow with evidence. If a sentence can be cut, cut it. If a word can be removed, remove it.

FORMATTING_RULES

UI labels: Space Mono, UPPER_SNAKE_CASE.
Headings: Inter 400, sentence case, tight tracking.
Body: Inter 400, secondary color, 16-18px.
Numbers: Always formatted ($12.4M, 8.2% APY).

WRITE_THIS
  • "Professionally managed vaults delivering consistent yield."
  • "$24M TVL across 4 vaults. 100% uptime since launch."
  • "Fully audited ERC-4626 infrastructure."
NOT_THIS
  • "The most amazing DeFi yields you've ever seen!"
  • "Join thousands of happy investors! 🚀"
  • "Revolutionary next-gen blockchain technology."
